# Sales Application - User Manual

Welcome to the Sales Management System. This guide will help you navigate and use the core features of the application.

---

## 1. Dashboard Overview
The dashboard provides a high-level summary of your business:
- **Lifetime Sales**: Total revenue generated from all bills.
- **Outstanding**: Total amount receivable (Opening Balances + Bills - Payments).
- **Items Sold**: Total count of all items sold.
- **Total Customers**: Total active customer base.
- **Charts**: Visual representation of monthly sales and cash flow.

## 2. Managing Customers
- **Add Customer**: Go to `General > Customers` and click "Add Customer". Ensure you enter an accurate "Opening Balance" if they have previous debt.
- **Customer Ledger**: View a detailed transaction history for any customer by selecting them from the list.

## 3. Inventory Management
- **Items**: Manage your products under `General > Items`. Set prices and track category-wise performance.

## 4. Sales and Billing
- **Create Bill**: Navigate to `Finance > Create Bill`. Select a customer, add items, and the system will automatically calculate totals.
- **Future Dates**: The system restricts bills to the current or past dates to ensure reporting integrity.
- **Printing**: You can print professional invoices directly from the bill view.

## 5. Payments and Collections
- **Record Payment**: When a customer pays, go to `Finance > Payments` and click "New Payment".
- **Outstanding Sync**: Payments recorded here will immediately reduce the "Outstanding" amount on the dashboard.

## 6. Financial Reports
Access specialized reports under the `Reports` menu:
- **Sales Report**: Filter sales by date range or customer.
- **Cash Statement**: Complete overview of cash flow and receivables.
- **Third Party Ledger**: Track payments and flow for external partners.

## 7. Configuration
- **Company Settings**: Update your business name, logo, and currency symbol under `Settings > Company Settings`.
- **User Profile**: Change your name or password from the top-right profile menu.
