# Sales Management System

A professional, enterprise-grade Sales and Financial Management Application built with Laravel. This system tracks customers, inventory, billing, and complex financial ledgers including third-party payments and commissions.

## Key Features
- **Modern Dashboard**: Dynamic statistics cards and real-time financial charts.
- **Billing System**: Professional invoice generation and print-ready bills.
- **Ledger Management**: Automated tracking of customer balances, opening balances, and payments.
- **Third-Party Flow**: Advanced tracking of commissions and third-party financial adjustments.
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive sales, cash flow, and statement reports.
- **Security**: Strict date validation (no future-dating) and role-based access.
